About Me

Hi my name is Nathan Harris and I created Borrow This ! as a Capstone Project for General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive. I come from the entertainment industry where I worked as a lighting console programming and carried with me a fascination for the systems that make movies. Now that fascination as shifted as I change focus from the big screen to the computer screen, enjoying all the complexities herein. The idea of this project came from my friends always asking me to borrow a camping tent or my camera and my desire to help people use my film equipment. I quickly realized that there are all kinds of items that people might be willing to rent and wouldn't it be cool if there was ONE place to view them? I built this project with full stack Python Django, PostgreSQL for the database, and Bulma for the CSS Library. I am really proud of this project as I tried to cram as many features as I could in one week. I hope you get to enjoy it!
